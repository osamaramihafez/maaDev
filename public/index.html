We Aim to Build Bridges Through Sport.

The Muslim Athletic Association is an organization dedicated to bringing a competitive athletic edge to the community while holding tight to Muslim values and ethics.
We focus on three things:

  • Building fruitful relationships within the community.
  • Valuing the importance of individual wellness.
  • Producing positively competitive mindsets.

These are not easy tasks. It takes a village! We wouldn't exists without the wonderful community that we serve and belong to. Through our programs and events, we wish to be a social get away from negative influences and a gateway to positive ones.

Active Clubs

*COVID-Compatible! We still host these programs free of charge!*

The MAA running club meets up every Wednesday at new locations in Mississauga and the GTA. We'll soon be offering more:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Hiking

Running, Cycling, and Hiking in a group is much easier than without. We push and encourage each other to go further, while catering to different skill levels. This is something we can all use to gain better habits and have small social interactions during this time. We encourage you to come try it out and bring a friend! Contact info@maaweb.org to get involved.

ISNA Canada

Putting Faith Into Action

ISNA Canada is a wonderful organization that provides a multitude of much needed services to the community. Having recently pinpointed their focus on youth, ISNA Canada has partnered with MAA to mesh the benefits of faith and action through sport.

Human Concern International

Empower Individuals and Communities Towards a Brighter Future

HCI is a Canadian based Muslim organization focused on ending global poverty and saving lives. They aim to protect and improve the lives of vulnerable people all around the world through community efforts.

Naseeha

Just a Call or Text Away

7 days a week Naseeha answers calls from around the world from Muslims and non-muslims. They has been a great bridge for individuals who are going through life’s challenges. Where hope is lost, Naseeha is found. We value our partnership with them.
