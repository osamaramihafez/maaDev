
Active Clubs Agreement

- The information that I have provided in this form is accurate to the best of my knowledge.
- The Muslim Athletic Association, hereby to be referenced as "MAA", Naseeha, and Human Concern International shall all be referenced as "the organizing partners". 
- None of the organizing partners are responsible for any loss, injury, harm, or sickness that I may experience as a result of any activity that I choose to participate in.
- The MAA is allowed to keep track of my name and information for contact and reference purposes.
- I will not intentionally cause harm to anyone involved in any MAA activities.
- I understand that if I choose to participate, I do so at my own risk.
- I will abide by all government recommended COVID-19 protocols to the best of my ability.
- I will abide by up to date laws and guidelines pertaining to public gatherings found in O. Reg. 52/20: ORDER UNDER SUBSECTION 7.0.2 (4) OF THE ACT - ORGANIZED PUBLIC EVENTS, CERTAIN GATHERINGS  [1].
- I will ensure that I keep a safe distance of two meters between myself and other participants as recommended by health Canada.
- If I feel sick or suspect any symptoms of flu or Covid-19, I will not participate. 
- If I encounter any participants to have the symptoms or know of pre-existing conditions, I will immediately inform MAA staff. 
- I will not shake hands or touch others.
- Any breach of the above conditions will result in immediate removal of myself from the participant's group.

[1] O. Reg. 52/20: ORDER UNDER SUBSECTION 7.0.2 (4) OF THE ACT - ORGANIZED PUBLIC EVENTS, CERTAIN GATHERINGS can be found at https://www.ontario.ca/laws/regulation/200052.
